Video Games and the perfect learning model

January 8th, 2022

Game designers have utilized the game play loop to master the art of educating players how to learn intricate mechanics in a fun engaging way for hundreds of years. Complex games such as Chess, Go, onto Souls games, Civlization, World of Warcraft enable the player to achieve their own levels of success based on repition, easily reognizable win / lose criteria, and structured progress.
